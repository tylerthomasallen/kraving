Description

Shakshuka is a tomato stew with poached eggs in it.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Eggs
  • Chili Pepper
  • Onions
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 375
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet. Add onions and peppers. Cook for 20min.
  3. Gently crack eggs into skillet over tomato. Season with salt and pepper. Tranfer to oven and bake 7 minutes.
